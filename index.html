My Story

I was born on April 7th, 1999, in Granada, and I've been fascinated by video games since childhood. During my adolescence, I developed a strong interest in history, dedicating years to studying it.

The most valuable skill I gained was critical thinking and the ability to question what we take for granted—something I now apply in my work as a Quality Assurance Tester. What motivates me the most is knowing that my work helps ensure the final product reaches players in the most polished state possible.
